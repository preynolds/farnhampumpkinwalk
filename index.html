A "FUN"-raiser put on by Farnham's Home and School Club, the Pumpkin Walk is a freakishly fun Halloween event for kids and adults alike! There will be a pumpkin patch, haunted house, carnival games, inflatables, food, a bake sale, costume contests and so much more!

When:
Friday October 26th, 4-8pm

Where:
Farnham Elementary School, 15711 Woodard Road, San Jose, CA 95124
